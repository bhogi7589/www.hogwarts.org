What's New

  • Practicals Re-Introduced in Defense Against the Dark Arts Classes

    As we all know, Professor Dolores Umbridge (High Inquisitor) removed practials from the subject of Defense Against the Dark Arts. That's why we have expelled her so that we can re-introduce practicals to the subject because it is important that we (and you) know that our students can apply their knowledge in real life.

  • Transfiguration Discovery

    Transfiguration helps in all. Therefore, we must match the latest trends of transfiguration. In that process, we discovered a technique through which even the toughest transfigurations could be done in the simplest ways. Now, we are introducing it little by little to our students according to their level.
